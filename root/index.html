URBAN

The Urban Arena is designed to look like a small street area with cars, crates, and narrow paths. It creates fast action where players need quick reactions and good movement. There are many places to hide, push forward, or surprise other players, which keeps every match exciting. This arena can hold up to 12 players and is great for short games that focus on fast battles.

This arena has a £20 base fee, plus £15 for each player. For enquiries, please email aberdeen@rapidfirepaintball.co.uk

FOREST

The Forest Arena is a large outdoor space with trees, bushes, and natural cover. It gives players more room to move and encourages slower, more tactical play. The hidden paths and open areas make it good for planning ambushes or working together as a team. It can support up to 16 players and offers a more strategic style of gameplay.

This arena has a £20 base fee, plus £15 for each player. For enquiries, please email aberdeen@rapidfirepaintball.co.uk

Price : £20.00

INDOOR

The Indoor Arena is inside a warehouse and includes many obstacles, barriers, and tight corners. It creates quick and intense matches where players need fast thinking and good aim. Because it is indoors, it works well in any weather and keeps the action steady. This arena fits up to 10 players and is ideal for short games that focus on close battles.

This arena has a £20 base fee, plus £15 for each player. For enquiries, please email aberdeen@rapidfirepaintball.co.uk
